About

I’m Zack Dinh, a geographer based in Long Beach, California. This site is a home for my work and interests across geography, woodworking, sailing, food systems, and the projects that shape my days.

I like building practical things, learning through making, and finding ways to connect landscape, craft, and technology. The pages here are a snapshot of what I’m working on and what I care about.
