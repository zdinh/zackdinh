Arques School

In the summer of 2025 I received instruction in traditional boatbuilding at the Arques School of Traditional Boatbuilding in Sausalito, CA. The mission of the school is to restore and replicate sailing vessels of historical significance to the San Francisco Bay. This opportunity was made possible through the Cerritos College Woodworking Manufacturing Technologies Ambassador Scholarship.
