California Relief Map Frame

Poplar and teak.

As a geographer and lifelong Californian, this piece has a very special place in my heart. The relief map is a Hubbard Scientific 1st edition from 1993. I found it outside of San Francisco City College and it holds the record for the coolest thing I have found on the side of the road. The frame features splined corners and an inner frame made of teak salvaged from my Lido 14 sailing dinghy.
